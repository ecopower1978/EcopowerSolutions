FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ varies by product line: Power Stations: 50 units; Solar Lights: 100 units; Flashlights: 200 units; Drones: 20 units (consumer) / 5 units (industrial); Circuit Breakers: 10 units. OEM/ODM projects may require higher MOQs — contact us for details.

Do you offer OEM / private label services?

Yes, OEM and ODM services are available for all product lines. We can customize logos, colors, packaging, product specifications, firmware, and more. A dedicated OEM project manager will be assigned to your account.

What certifications do your products carry?

Our products carry CE, RoHS, FCC, UN38.3, and ISO 9001 certifications as standard. Additional certifications (SAA for Australia, PSE for Japan, UKCA for UK) can be arranged. Certificate documents are available upon request.

How do you handle shipping and customs?

We support all major shipping terms: EXW, FOB Shenzhen, CIF, and DDP. We work with trusted freight forwarders for sea, air, and express shipping. Full export documentation — commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin — is provided for every shipment.

Can we get product samples before placing a bulk order?

Yes. Standard product samples can be ordered at cost (sample fee refundable on first bulk order). Sample delivery takes 5–7 business days via express courier. Custom OEM samples take 10–15 business days.

What payment terms do you accept?

Standard payment terms are 30% T/T deposit with balance before shipment. For established clients, we offer Net 30 and L/C at sight. We accept T/T bank transfer, PayPal (samples only), and trade assurance via Alibaba.
