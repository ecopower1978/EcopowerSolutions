Who We Are

A Factory Built on Innovation & Quality

CeoPower Solution Co., Ltd. is a vertically integrated manufacturer specializing in new-energy consumer and industrial electronics. We operate a 20,000 m² ISO 9001-certified manufacturing facility in the Longhua District of Shenzhen — one of the world's most advanced electronics manufacturing hubs.

Our vertically integrated production covers R&D, component procurement, SMT assembly, battery cell integration, quality inspection, and export packaging — all under one roof. This gives us unmatched control over quality and lead times.

We serve importers, distributors, e-commerce aggregators, and retail chains across North America, Europe, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and Australia. OEM and ODM partnerships are a cornerstone of our business.

Compliance

Certifications & Standards

All our products are rigorously tested and certified to meet international standards for safety, EMC, and environmental compliance.

CE

CE Mark

European Conformity — indicates compliance with EU health, safety, and environmental protection standards. Required for all products sold in the European Economic Area.

RoHS

RoHS Directive

Restriction of Hazardous Substances — confirms our products contain no restricted heavy metals or chemicals. EU Directive 2011/65/EU compliant across all product lines.

FCC

FCC Certification

U.S. Federal Communications Commission approval for radio frequency emissions and electromagnetic interference. Required for all electronic devices sold in the USA.

UN38.3

UN 38.3 Battery

United Nations transport testing standard for lithium batteries. Ensures our battery products are safe for air, sea, and ground shipping worldwide.

ISO
9001

ISO 9001:2015

International Quality Management System certification. Our entire manufacturing and supply chain operations are audited annually by a third-party registrar.

SRRC
CCC

SRRC & CCC

State Radio Regulation of China and China Compulsory Certificate. Covers drone RF transmissions and electrical equipment sold in the Chinese domestic market.

Our Journey

12 Years of Growth

From a team of 8 engineers to a workforce of 450+ — here are the milestones that shaped CeoPower Solution.

2012

Company Founded

CeoPower Solution established in Shenzhen with 8 employees and a 500m² workshop, focused on LED flashlight manufacturing.

2015

Solar Product Line Launched

Expanded into solar-powered garden and street lights. Secured first export contract to European distributors.

2017

ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 quality management system certification. Moved to new 8,000m² facility.

2019

Portable Power Station Launch

Launched our flagship LiFePO₄ portable power station line. Annual revenue exceeded $15M for the first time.

2021

Drone & Electrical Division

Added commercial UAV and air circuit breaker product lines. Expanded factory to current 20,000m².

2024

500+ Global B2B Clients

Reached milestone of 500 active wholesale clients across 50+ countries. Launched dedicated OEM/ODM service division.
